About the data
Our dataset is from USDAâ€™s National Household Food Acquisition and Purchase Survey (FoodAPS), which is a nationally representative survey of 4,826 American households about household food purchases and acquisitions between April 2012 and January 2013. Stratified sampling was implemented to collect the data and the stratification was based on households enrolled in the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) and households income, with households receiving SNAP, non-SNAP household with income less than 100% of the Federal Poverty Guideline (FPG), non-SNAP households with income between 100% and 185% of the FPG, and non-SNAP households with more than 185% of the FPG.
About the visualization
In this visualization, we aim to explore what and how a variety of factors impact on an individual's Body Mass Index (BMI). We selected 12 factors of interest and visualized people's responses to different factors among participants who are not overweight and participants who are overweight or obese.
